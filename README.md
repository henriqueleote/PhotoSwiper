# PhotoSwiper

An iOS app for quickly cleaning up your camera roll. Swipe through your photos one at a time —
swipe right to keep, swipe left to mark for deletion — and clear out unwanted photos in a fast,
Tinder-style review flow.

## Features

- Review photos **old to new** or in **random** order
- Swipe right (or tap ❤️) to keep a photo, swipe left (or tap ❌) to mark it for deletion
- Liked photos are remembered locally so they won't show up again in future sessions
- Batch-delete all photos marked for deletion at the end of a session, with a confirmation step
- "Restore liked photos" option to reset your review history

## Built With

- SwiftUI
- Photos framework (`PHAsset` / `PHPhotoLibrary`)
- RealmSwift for local storage of liked photos
